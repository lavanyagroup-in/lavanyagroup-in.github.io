About the Us

Divine Artistry Since 1992
30+Years of
Trust

Crafting Sacred Memories
Since 1992

Established in 1992 at the foothills of Tirumala, Lavanya's Pictures & Frames is a leading manufacturer of divine art, founded by the visionary Sri. P. Venkateswarlu. We operate a state-of-the-art printing press that gives life to fine art and religious imagery with unmatched detail.

Dedicated to the unique approach of creating art deeply rooted in Indian spirituality, our studio is committed to excellence—producing pieces that not only beautify spaces but also serve as a profound connection to divinity.
